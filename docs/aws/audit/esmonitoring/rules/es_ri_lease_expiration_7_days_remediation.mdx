### Remediation

How to renew ElasticSearch reserved instances which are expiring in 7 days?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console.
2. Open the Amazon Elasticsearch Service console.
3. Navigate to the "Reserved instances" page.
   (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "Elasticsearch Reserved Instance Lease Expiration In The Next 7 Days" Policy.)
4. Review the list of reserved instances in your account and identify the ones that are expiring in 7 days.
5. Select the reserved instance that you want to renew by checking the box next to it.
6. Click on the "Actions" button and choose "Renew reserved instance" from the dropdown menu.
7. Review the details of the reservation you are renewing, including the instance type, term, and pricing.
8. Select the desired term length for the renewed reservation. You can choose to renew for another one or three-year term.
9. Click on the "Renew" button to initiate the renewal process.
10. Review the confirmation message and ensure that the renewed reservation matches your requirements.
11. Wait for the renewal process to complete. This may take a few moments.
