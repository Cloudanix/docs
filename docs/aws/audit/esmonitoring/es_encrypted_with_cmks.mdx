---
title: Elasticsearch Domain Should Be Encrypted with KMS CMKs
---

### More Info:

Your Amazon ElasticSearch (ES) domains should be encrypted with KMS Customer Master Keys (CMKs) instead of AWS managed-keys

### Risk Level

High

### Address

Security

### Compliance Standards

GDPR, APRA, MAS, NIST
