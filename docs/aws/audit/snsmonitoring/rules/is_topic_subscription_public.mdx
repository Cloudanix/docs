### More Info:

Your AWS Simple Notification Service (SNS) topics should not allow "Everyone" to subscribe in order to protect the messages published to your topics against attackers or unauthorized personnel.

### Risk Level

Medium

### Address

Security

### Compliance Standards

HITRUST, AWSWAF, SOC2, NISTCSF, PCIDSS

### Remediation

How to update the access control policies attached to the SNS topics that are publicly accessible?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console using an account with administrative privileges.
2. Open the SNS (Simple Notification Service) console by navigating to the SNS service.
    (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "SNS Topics Should Not Allow Global Subscribe" Policy.)
3. Select the publicly accessible SNS topic for which you want to update the access control policy.
4. In the topic details page, click on the "Access policy" tab.
5. Click on the "Edit" button to modify the access policy for the topic.
6. In the access policy editor, you can make the necessary changes to the access control policy. Carefully review and update the policy based on your security requirements. You can modify the "Principal" elements, "Condition" elements, or "Action" elements to restrict access or specify the desired permissions.
7. After making the required changes, click on the "Save changes" button to apply the updated policy.
