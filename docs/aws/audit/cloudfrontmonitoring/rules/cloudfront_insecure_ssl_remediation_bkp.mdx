### Remediation

How to remove the deprecated SSLv3 protocol from your Cloudfront distributions origin?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Open the CloudFront console:
   - Sign in to the AWS Management Console.
   - Go to the CloudFront service.
     (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "CloudFront Distributions Should Not Use Insecure SSL Protocols" Policy.)
2. Select the desired CloudFront distribution:
   - Locate and select the CloudFront distribution for which you want to remove the SSLv3 protocol.
3. Edit the distribution:
   - Click on the "Distribution Settings" tab.
   - Click on the "Edit" button to modify the distribution settings.
4. Update the origin settings:
   - Scroll down to the "Origins and Origin Groups" section.
   - Select the origin for which you want to remove the SSLv3 protocol.
   - Click on the "Edit" button next to the selected origin.
5. Configure SSL/TLS settings:
   - In the "Origin Settings" section, scroll down to the "Origin Protocol Policy" option.
   - Choose the appropriate policy that supports modern, secure SSL/TLS protocols (such as TLSv1.2 or higher).
   - Ensure that SSLv3 is not selected or allowed.
6. Save the changes:
   - Click on the "Save Changes" button to update the origin settings.
