### Remediation

How to prohibit Event bus from Cross account access?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Open the AWS Management Console and navigate to the EventBridge service.
2. Click on "Event Buses" in the navigation panel on the left side.
    (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "Event Bus Should Not Be Exposed" Policy.)
3. Identify the event bus for which you want to disable cross-account access. Take note of its name.
4. Open the AWS Management Console and navigate to the IAM service.
5. Click on "Policies" in the navigation panel on the left side.
6. Search for and select the policy that allows cross-account access to the event bus.
7. Click on the "Delete Policy" button.
8. A confirmation dialog will appear. Review the information and click on the "Delete" button to remove the policy.
9. Return to the EventBridge service in the AWS Management Console.
10. Click on "Event Buses" in the navigation panel on the left side.
11. Select the event bus for which you want to disable cross-account access.
12. Click on the "Actions" dropdown and select "Edit" to modify the event bus settings.
13. In the "Edit event bus" window, deselect the option to "Allow external accounts to send events to this event bus".
14. Click on the "Save" button to apply the changes.
15. Review the other event bus settings and ensure they align with your desired configuration.
