title: RDS Reserved Instances Should Not Have Status - Payment Pending
---

### More Info:

Identify any pending RDS Reserved Instance (RI) purchases available within your AWS account. A payment-pending RDS RI purchase is a reservation purchase that cannot be fully processed due to issues with the payment method

### Risk Level

High

### Address

Cost Optimisation

### Compliance Standards

CBP

### Remediation

How to identify any pending RDS Reserved Instance (RI) purchases within your AWS account?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Open the AWS Support Console:
   - Go to the AWS Support Center at https://console.aws.amazon.com/support/.
   - Sign in with your AWS account credentials.
2. Create a new support case:
   - Click on the "Create case" button to initiate a new support case.
   - Select the appropriate support plan and case type.
3. Provide the necessary details:
   - In the case creation form, provide accurate and detailed information about the pending payment for your RDS reserved instance.
   - Specify the specific reserved instance for which the payment is pending.
     (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "RDS Reserved Instances Should Not Have Status - Payment Pending" Policy.)
   - Include any relevant details such as the reservation ID, payment method, and any actions you have already taken to resolve the issue.
4. Select the appropriate severity level:
   - Choose the severity level that aligns with the impact of the payment pending issue on your operations.
   - Provide a clear explanation of the impact the pending payment has on your RDS environment.
5. Submit the support case:
   - Review the case details to ensure accuracy and completeness.
   - Click on the "Submit" or "Create case" button to submit the support case to AWS Support.
6. Monitor the support case:
   - Once the case is submitted, AWS Support will review and process your request.
   - Monitor the case in the AWS Support Console for updates, notifications, or requests for additional information.
7. Collaborate with AWS Support:
   - Respond promptly to any requests or inquiries from AWS Support regarding the pending payment issue.
   - Provide any necessary documentation or information requested by AWS Support.
8. Follow the guidance from AWS Support:
   - AWS Support will provide guidance on how to resolve the pending payment issue.
   - Follow their instructions and take appropriate actions to resolve the issue.
