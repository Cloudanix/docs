---
title: EC2 Instance Snapshots Should Not Be Public
---

### More Info:

Your EC2 instance snapshots should not be publicly accessible. This is to avoid exposing your private data.

### Risk Level

High

### Address

Security

### Compliance Standards

NIST, PCIDSS, ARPA, MAS
