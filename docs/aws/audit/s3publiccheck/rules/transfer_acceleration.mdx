---
title: S3 Buckets Should Use Transfer Acceleration
---

### More Info:

S3 buckets should be using Transfer Acceleration feature to increase the speed (up to 500%) of data transfers in and out of Amazon S3 using AWS edge network.

### Risk Level

Low

### Address

Operational Maturity

### Compliance Standards

CBP

### Remediation

How to enable transfer acceleration for S3 buckets?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Open the AWS Management Console and navigate to the Amazon S3 service.
2. Click on the name of the existing S3 bucket for which you want to enable Transfer Acceleration.
    (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "S3 Buckets Should Use Transfer Acceleration" Policy.)
3. In the bucket details page, click on the "Properties" tab.
4. Under the "Transfer acceleration" section, click on the "Edit" button.
5. In the "Transfer acceleration" window, select the option for "Enable" to enable Transfer Acceleration for the bucket.
6. Click on the "Save changes" button to enable Transfer Acceleration.
7. Once Transfer Acceleration is enabled, a new URL will be provided for accelerated transfers. This URL can be used to upload or download objects to/from the bucket.
8. Update your application or client to utilize the new Transfer Acceleration URL for uploading or downloading objects. The format of the Transfer Acceleration URL is typically in the form of `bucketname.s3-accelerate.amazonaws.com`.
9. Test the Transfer Acceleration functionality by uploading or downloading objects using the new URL. You should experience improved data transfer speeds compared to the standard S3 endpoints.
10. Monitor the Transfer Acceleration performance and consider benchmarking to assess the improvement in data transfer speeds.
