### More Info:

You should not have unused Elastic Load Balancers in your AWS account. Unused ELBs should be deleted to help lower the cost of your monthly AWS bill.

### Risk Level

Low

### Address

Cost Optimisation

### Compliance Standards

HITRUST, SOC2, NISTCSF
