---
title: CloudTrails Must Log Management Events
---

### More Info:

All your AWS CloudTrail trails should be configured to log Management events in order to record important operations such as EC2 RunInstances, DescribeInstances, TerminateInstances and Console Login.

### Risk Level

Medium

### Address

Security

### Compliance Standards

SOC2, GDPR, PCIDSS, NIST

### Remediation

How to enable Management events for all CloudTrail trails inside AWS account?

#### Using AWS Console

1. Open the AWS Management Console and navigate to the CloudTrail service.
2. Click on the "Trails" menu on the left side.
    (In the Cloudanix Console, navigate to "Misconfig" page and look for Affected Assets for "CloudTrails Must Log Management Events" Policy.)
3. In the list of trails, select each trail one by one.
4. Click on the trail name to access the trail configuration.
5. In the trail configuration page, click on the "Edit" button.
6. Scroll down to the "Event logging" section.
7. In the "Management events" subsection, toggle the switch to enable management events.
8. Save the changes by clicking on the "Save" button.
9. Repeat steps 3-8 for each CloudTrail trail in your AWS account.
