---
title: Service Accounts Should Not Have Admin Privileges
---

### More Info:

Ensuring that no service accounts have admin privileges.

### Risk Level

High

### Address

Security

### Compliance Standards

CBP, CIS

### Remediation

How to disable service account having admin privileges.

#### Using GCP Console

1. Log in to the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Console using your GCP account credentials.
2. Navigate to the IAM & Admin section by selecting "IAM & Admin" from the main menu.
3. In the IAM & Admin page, select "Service Accounts" to manage service account roles and permissions.
4. Locate the service account that has admin privileges assigned.
5. Click on the service account to view its assigned roles and permissions.
6. Identify the admin roles assigned to the service account.
7. To remove the admin privileges:
   - Click on the "Edit" button next to the service account.
   - Find the admin roles in the service account's assigned roles.
   - Click on the "X" icon or remove button next to each admin role to remove them.
8. Review the remaining roles assigned to the service account and ensure they are necessary and appropriate for its intended purpose.
9. Click on the "Save" button to apply the changes to the service account's roles and permissions.
