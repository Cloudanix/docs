---
title: "Ad-hoc running an Audit on a Policy"
description: "You can reevaluate a policy on demand"
---

Here is how you do it.

1. From your Audit Dashboard, go to the Misconfiguration details page. E.g.

<img src="/images/select-misconfig.png" />

2. From the Misconfig page select Risks.

<img src="/images/select-risks.png" />

3. Click on any policy, on the slide out you will see a Reevaluate option. If you click on the option it will schedule the Run right away. In few seconds your Dashboard should refresh with results

<img src="/images/reevaluate.png" />
